<objective>
Fix the company-view Spotify Streams aggregation bug (D-19, D-20, D-21). The current SSR transform in `app/(dashboard)/page.tsx` iterates ALL keys of `spotifyTracksRaw` without filtering, so playlist entities (which `processSpotifyTracks` flattens together with performers under each company) inflate the streams total. The client reported ~29M on the "Camping" company ā€” mathematically impossible without playlist tracks.

Purpose: Company streams total reflects real performer contribution only.
Output: `rankingsByPerformer` and `allTracks` in `spotifyData` contain only real performer keys, sourced from `CompanyInfo.performers`.
</objective>
